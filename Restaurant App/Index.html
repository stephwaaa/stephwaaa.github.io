About Us



Marco's Italian has been open since 1998. Born in Italy, Marco and his family had the dream to bring their authentic italian flavours to the USA.
With that dream realized, Marco and his family have delighted their customers for years and will for years to come.
Please come in and join us for a meal, and experience an authentic taste of Italy.
